The Problem

Global payments are broken

Sending money across borders is slow, expensive, opaque, and requires trusting a middleman with your funds. Crowdfunding platforms take 5–12% cuts, hold your money for weeks, and can freeze campaigns.

🐌
Slow Settlement
International bank wires take 3–5 business days and often fail silently.
💸
Hidden Fees
$20–$50 per international transfer. 3–5% FX markups. Crowdfunding takes 5–12% cuts.
🔐
No Transparency
You can't verify where your money is, who holds it, or when it will settle.

The Solution

Send money beyond borders

StellarFlow is a non-custodial dApp that combines instant XLM payments with fully on-chain crowdfunding powered by Soroban smart contracts — no backend, no custodian, no surprises.

⚡
Instant Finality
Stellar reaches consensus in ~5 seconds. Payments are final, no rollbacks.
🦋
Freighter Wallet
Connect your Freighter wallet and sign every transaction locally. We never see your keys.
📜
Soroban Contracts
Crowdfunding lives 100% on-chain. Funds are locked in a Rust smart contract — verifiable on the ledger.
🏅
Loyalty Tiers
Cross-contract DonorBadge awards Bronze / Silver / Gold tiers atomically with every donation.
